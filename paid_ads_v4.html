Critical Issues
🚨
SAT Conversion Rate Collapsed — 11.32% → 5.07% (−6.25pp)
SAT leads grew 62% YoY (167K→293K) but conversions dropped 20% (18,676→14,836). Spend up 17.9%. Lead quality or post-lead funnel issue. Audit CRM, landing pages, lead scoring, counsellor capacity.
🚨
VP Lead Volume Down 42% YoY — CPL Worsened ₹313 → ₹396
VP leads fell from 271K to 157K despite only 27% spend cut. Conversion rate also under pressure. Volume recovery is #1 priority for VP admission targets — new creative, fresh audiences needed urgently.
🚨
YouTube CPT ₹1,56,203 — 66× Worse than Search Brand (₹2,347)
YouTube: ₹85.9L for 55 conversions. Demand Gen: CPT ₹26,155. Combined ₹2.69Cr for 756 conversions. Immediate reallocation to Search Brand expected to generate ~600 additional conversions.

Pmax (CPT ₹7,271) is dragging NSAT efficiency. NSAT Search Brand CPT ₹2,065 is 3.5× better. Shift NSAT budget mix toward Search Brand.
⚠️
SAT Phase 2 CPL ₹296 — 78% Higher than Phase 1 (₹167)
Phase 3 (62% of SAT budget) CPL ₹206 vs Phase 1 ₹167. Move 15–20% from Phase 2/3 to Phase 1 and test efficiency at scale.
⚠️
Facebook Conv% Only 3.33% — CPT ₹7,446
Facebook drives 20% of leads but 3.33% conversion. Use as awareness/lead gen only — pair with Search Brand for conversion capture.

₹53.7Cr spend, 287K leads, 22,866 conversions. Every ₹1Cr moved from YouTube/DGen to Search Brand adds ~425 conversions at current CPT.
